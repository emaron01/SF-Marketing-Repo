About Erik

Why SalesForecaster.io Exists

For more than two decades, I’ve led sales teams through every kind of forecast cycle imaginable. And one truth became impossible to ignore: we waste countless hours in forecast calls trying to piece together incomplete information, when that time should be spent coaching reps and advancing deals.

Inconsistent qualification. Missed risks. Different reps interpreting MEDDPICC in different ways. Leaders forced to make decisions without a clear, unified view of the business.

I built SalesForecaster.io to fix this.

Drawing on years of MEDDPICC training and frontline sales leadership, I designed a platform that brings structure, consistency, and evidence to every deal review. Whether you lead one team or many, every AI-driven review surfaces the truth of the deal—what’s strong, what’s missing, and where coaching will have the biggest impact.

Matthew enforces consistent qualification—even if your team isn't formally MEDDPICC trained.

The result is simple: more clarity, more confidence, and more closed revenue.

— Erik Maroney, Founder
