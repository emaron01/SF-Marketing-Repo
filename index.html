The Problem

Forecast Calls Are Broken

Sales leaders don't have time to run deep forecast calls with every rep. Reps get rushed. Risks get missed. Forecasts become guesswork. And coaching gets pushed aside for pipeline housekeeping.

⏱

No Time for Deep Reviews

Leaders spend forecast calls gathering information instead of coaching. Every minute chasing updates is a minute not spent moving deals forward.

🎯

Rep Optimism ≠ Accuracy

Reps tell leaders what they want to hear. Missing champions, weak metrics, and paper process risk stay buried until it's too late to act.

📊

CRM Data Is Half the Story

Most forecasting tools rely on CRM data — but CRM data is only as good as rep input. Garbage in, garbage out. Real accuracy needs structured rep interviews.

🏋️

Evidence-Based Health Score

A Health Score Built on
What Reps Actually Know

Most health scores are calculated from CRM fields. SalesForecaster.io's health score is different — it's built entirely from Matthew's structured deal interviews. Every score is backed by evidence, classified by strength, and updated with every review.

🔬

Evidence-Classified Scoring

Every category scores 0–3 based on evidence strength: Unknown → Vague Assertion → Credible Indirect → Explicit Verified. No guessing, no rep optimism.

📈

Updates With Deal Progression

The health score adjusts with every Matthew review. Leaders watch scores move in real-time as reps gather stronger evidence — or see scores stall when reps aren't progressing deals.

🎯

Coaching to Specific Actions

Because each score is backed by specific evidence, leaders can coach reps on exactly what to do next — not just "get a stronger champion" but "get the buyer to confirm the criteria in writing."
