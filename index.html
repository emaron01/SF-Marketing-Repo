Evidence-Based Health Score

A Health Score Built on
What Reps Actually Know

Most health scores are calculated from CRM fields. SalesForecaster.io's health score is built entirely from Matthew's structured deal interviews. Every score is backed by evidence, classified by strength, and updated with every review.

🔬

Evidence-Classified Scoring

Every category scores 0–3 based on evidence strength: Unknown → Vague Assertion → Credible Indirect → Explicit Verified. No guessing, no rep optimism.

📈

Updates With Deal Progression

The health score adjusts with every Matthew review. Leaders watch scores move in real-time as reps gather stronger evidence — or see scores stall when deals aren't progressing.

🎯

Coaching to Specific Actions

A rep says "we need 4 signatures through their Ariba system." Sounds solid. But Matthew surfaces that the rep still needs to identify who signs and how long it takes — because process risk kills deals late. Leaders coach the specific gap, not a gut feeling.
